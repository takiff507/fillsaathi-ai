# FillSaathi AI

FillSaathi AI is a simple form help website that explains confusing form fields in easy language.

## What it does

- Explains what each form field means
- Tells the user what to write and where to write it
- Warns users to fill private details manually
- Gives a final checklist before submitting a form
- Helps with both physical and online forms

## Product idea

FillSaathi AI is made for people who get confused while filling bank forms, KYC forms, college forms, job forms, loan forms, insurance forms, and other official forms.

The goal is simple:

**Form samjho, safely bharo.**

## Local use

Open `index.html` in a browser.
